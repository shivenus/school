What are the differences between a linked list and a stack?

Linked list
data can be pulled from and inserted anywere in the list.

Stack
data has to be pulled from the top, the last one inserted, that's why it's a LIFO structure.


book response
It’s possible to insert a node anywhere in a linked list and remove a node from anywhere in a linked list.
However, nodes in a stack may be inserted only at the top of the stack and removed only from the top of a stack.
